DroneEar Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Overview

DroneEar is an acoustic drone detection application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how DroneEar handles your data.

Data Collection

DroneEar does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Specifically:

  • No audio recordings are stored or transmitted. Microphone data is processed in real-time on your device and immediately discarded.
  • No location data is transmitted. GPS coordinates are used locally for environment detection and map display only.
  • No Bluetooth or WiFi scan data is transmitted. Remote ID data is processed on-device only.
  • No analytics, crash reports, or telemetry are collected.
  • No accounts or registration required.
  • No cookies or tracking technologies used.

Device Permissions

DroneEar requests the following permissions, all processed locally on your device:

  • Microphone — Real-time acoustic analysis to identify drone sound patterns. Audio is never recorded or stored.
  • Location — Determines indoor/outdoor environment for detection sensitivity. Displays your position on the map. Never transmitted.
  • Bluetooth — Scans for drone Remote ID (ASTM F3411) BLE broadcasts. Data stays on-device.
  • WiFi (Android only) — Scans for drone Remote ID via WiFi Beacon and WiFi NAN. Data stays on-device.
  • Motion sensors — Compass heading for estimating sound source direction.

Local Storage

Detection history is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (MMKV). This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

DroneEar does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or data processing services. The app functions entirely offline.

Children's Privacy

DroneEar does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. Since the app collects no personal data from any user, no special provisions are necessary.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
